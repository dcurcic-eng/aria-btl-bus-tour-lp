BEFORE YOU BOARD

Quick questions.

Do I really need to RSVP if admission is free?
Admission is free, but the bus has a finite number of chairs. RSVP reserves your demo window so you walk on, not wait in line.
+
What happens in a five minute demo?
An Aria provider seats you on the device, explains what you'll feel, runs a short calibrated session, and answers questions. No commitment, no charge.
+
Can I demo more than one device tonight?
Yes. Most guests sample two or three. Reserve your top pick first, then drop by the desk on the bus for any open slots.
+
Parking, friends, food, what should I expect?
Park in the Aria lot or on the surrounding streets. Bring friends. Food trucks, music, and boutique pop ups fill the lot from 5 to 8 PM.
+
How does event only treatment pricing work?
Packages and single sessions booked on the night carry pricing you'll only see at this event. Aria's front desk on the bus has the full sheet.
